Choosing the Right Crust

Opt for healthier crust options like whole wheat, cauliflower, or almond flour crusts for added fiber and nutrients.

Nutritious Sauce Options

Homemade tomato sauce, pesto, or even hummus can make a great base without the extra sugar and sodium found in traditional sauces.

Healthy Toppings

  • Lean Proteins: Grilled chicken, turkey, tofu, or shrimp.
  • Veggies Galore: Bell peppers, spinach, mushrooms, onions, and more.
  • Healthy Cheeses: Try part-skim mozzarella, feta, or goat cheese.

Final Tips

Use less cheese, bake at high heat for a crispy crust, and enjoy in moderation with a side salad for a balanced meal.
