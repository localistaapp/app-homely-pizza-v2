Thai Mango Sticky Rice

A delicious and exotic Thai dessert made with sticky rice, coconut milk, and fresh mango slices.

Ingredients:

For the Sticky Rice:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For the Coconut Sauce:

  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional)

Toppings:

  • 1 ripe mango, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds or crushed peanuts

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak the glutinous rice for at least 3 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and steam the rice for 25-30 minutes until tender.
  3. Heat 1 cup coconut milk with sugar and salt until dissolved. Pour over the cooked rice and let it absorb for 20 minutes.
  4. For the topping sauce, heat ½ cup coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Optionally, add cornstarch mixed with water to thicken.
  5. Serve sticky rice with fresh mango slices, drizzle with coconut sauce, and sprinkle with sesame seeds or peanuts.

Enjoy this sweet and creamy Thai dessert!
